About Me

I started my college career as a Molecular Biology major because I am fascinated by the genetic code that composes all living things and the amazing things that scientists can do with genetic engineering. After 2 years of studying, I decided that working in a lab wasn’t really for me; so I switched gears and started to learn about a more easily manipulated code. That’s when I began the Software Development program.

I’ve always excelled in STEM subjects such as math and science, so it came as no surprise that I would enjoy the technical problem solving used in back-end development. But I quickly discovered that I also enjoy the artistic design required for front-end development. That is why my goal is to be a full-stack developer.
